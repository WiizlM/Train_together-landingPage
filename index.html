Working out together — never been easier.

Hello, we’re TrainTogether. Platform for people who want to have some company while training. We offer you an easy solution to find your training partners!

Anytime, anywhere

Accessible through our website or app, you can browse, attend or create training events at any time.

Communicate

Built-in live chat to conversate with your event attendants or groupmembers, you can share information quickly. Flexibility and communication are the key purposes of this platform.

Free of charge

Built for everyone, everywhere, it's a free to use platform. Attending events is free for everyone, these are YOUR events. Our platform just makes it easy for you to meet up :)

Fast and easy

App is easy to use. Just log in, choose the location and browse for training events. Join the event suitable for you and attend...it's that easy.
